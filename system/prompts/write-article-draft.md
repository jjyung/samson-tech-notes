# Write Article Draft

Use this prompt when the user asks to expand an idea or note into a technical article draft.

## Goal

Write a strong first draft that matches the repository's existing article style.

## Instructions

- Study the tone of files in `docs/articles/` before drafting.
- Keep the article argument-driven and insight-led.
- Prefer clear sectioning and progressive explanation.
- Avoid filler, marketing language, and exaggerated certainty.
- If examples or references are missing, leave the draft logically complete without fabricating specifics.

## Draft Principles

- Start from a real engineering tension or conceptual shift.
- Clarify what changes, why it matters, and what trade-off appears.
- End with a compact conclusion that states the main idea plainly.

## Path Preference

Default output path: `docs/articles/`
